MY FIRST DAY OF POGRAMMING

My html prgramming journey began when i entered level 100 in GCTU,in the year 2024. I started to learn progamming in html with the help of a website called free code camp. A lecturer in GCTU called Mr Danso was the one who introduced free code camp to us. When I started to visit this website i begun to think it was easy but litle did i know hard stuffs awaited for me . So therefore i started visiting tthis website day by day to earn points since our work will be be dependent on our point. “When I first discovered FreeCodeCamp, I was skeptical about learning coding online for free. However, the moment I started, I realized how structured and well-thought-out the platform is. I began with their Responsive Web Design course, and by the end of it, I had built projects that I could proudly showcase. What I love most about FreeCodeCamp is the hands-on approach. Each lesson encourages me to apply what I’ve learned immediately, which helps solidify my understanding. The supportive community is another huge plus. Whenever I get stuck, I know I can rely on their forums or even their Discord channels for help. Thanks to FreeCodeCamp, I’ve gained confidence in my coding abilities and even started contributing to open-source projects. It feels incredible to look back and see how far I’ve come, and I owe a big part of that journey to this amazing platform.”
